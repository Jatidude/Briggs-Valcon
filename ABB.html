Brand Description

ABB K-TEK LEVEL PRODUCTS offers one of the broadest range of level devices available in the marketplace. These technologies include: Magnetic Level Gauges, Dual Chamber Magnetic Level Gauges, Magnetostrictive Level Transmitters, Guided Wave Radar Level Transmitters, Laser Level Transmitters, Ultrasonic Level Transmitters, Buoyancy Level Switches, and Thermal Dispersion Level/Flow Switches. Our broad range of technologies enables us to provide an ideal solution for each level application.
