Brand Description

ValvTechnologies designs and manufactures valves specifically for the unique demands of power generation applications.

ValvTechnologies is the preeminent supplier of severe service valves for the fossil power industry. For more than 30 years, we have been solving the most challenging power plant issues including inability to isolate, erosion, leaking valves, packing problems, etc.

The standard operating conditions of power generation: high-temperatures, high-pressures, and at times, high-cycling, can severely impact the service life of a valve. Special consideration should be made in the selection of metallurgy, sealing design and operation. ValvTechnologies, a leading global manufacturer of metal-seated, severe service valves, has a proven performance record of providing field-tested, zero-leakage solutions.

Products

V1-1

The ValvTechnologies V1-1 design is the core valve by which all other metal seated ball valves are measured. The V1-1 features integral seats, HVOF RiTech™ hard coatings, a blow-out proof stem and live-loaded packing. The V1-1 is primarily used in high energy applications in the power industry and is available in sizes from 1/4” to 4” and in ASME/ANSI pressure classes 900 to 4500, including interpolated and special ratings. V1-1 valves are manufactured in a variety of forged materials and end connections to meet our customers’ specifications. Most importantly, every V1-1 valve for power industry applications is backed by the ValvTechnologies’ four-year zero-leakage guarantee.

IsoTech® Parallel Slide Gate Valves

IsoTech®, ValvTechnologies’ Parallel Slide Gate (PSG) valves, are the culmination of years of practical design enhancements and eliminating the limitations of typically accepted norms in gate valve design. The most advanced valve in its class, IsoTech is the proven solution for high temperature steam isolation applications in today’s modern combined cycle power plants. The design is Stellite-free and features a “through conduit” design for fully protected seating surfaces while in the open position. IsoTech® valves offer our rigorous four-year zero-leakage guarantee for power applications and low-cost maintainability.
